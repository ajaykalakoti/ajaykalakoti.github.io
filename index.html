01 — About

Building things that scale

I build the parts of a product that nobody sees and the parts everybody uses — and I like that the same person owns both.

I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Hyderabad, Telangana, currently at GreenAds Global where I build and maintain Telinfy, an enterprise CPaaS and omnichannel messaging platform.

My day-to-day spans the whole stack — designing RESTful APIs in Node.js and Express, modelling data in MySQL and PostgreSQL, wiring up RabbitMQ for high-throughput async processing, and building responsive client dashboards in Angular, React and Next.js.

I care about features that work end to end: from the database schema, through the API and message queue, to the button the client actually clicks. I've integrated the Meta WhatsApp Business API, JBM for RCS and SMS gateways, and debugged plenty of production delivery issues over SSH at 2am.

I work fast with modern AI tooling — Cursor, Claude Code and ChatGPT are part of my daily workflow for building, reviewing and debugging. Outside my full-time role I take on freelance projects and website builds.
